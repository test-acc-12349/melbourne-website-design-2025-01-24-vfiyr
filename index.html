Frequently Asked Questions

What's your typical project timeline?

Most projects are completed within 4-6 weeks. Complex e-commerce sites may take 8-10 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What's included in your fixed fee packages?

Our packages include custom design, responsive development, SEO optimization, content management system, basic content upload, and 30 days of post-launch support.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes, we offer flexible maintenance packages to keep your site secure, updated, and performing optimally. Ask about our monthly care plans.

Can you help with existing websites?

Absolutely! We offer redesign and optimization services for existing websites, including performance improvements and modern design updates.

What payment terms do you offer?

We typically require a 50% deposit to begin, with the remaining balance due upon project completion. We also offer flexible payment plans for larger projects.
